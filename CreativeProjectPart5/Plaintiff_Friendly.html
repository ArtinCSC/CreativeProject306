Plaintiff_Friendly Witness:   Babbu Singh, Roommate of Ethan Turner

  • Babbu Singh is a Punjabi American Citizen, who immigrated from New Delhi, India. He is currently a charcoal based artist, specializing in drawings. Babbu prides himself on breaking out from the traditional mold of occupations, and find happiness through drawing. He has been drawing since he was a child, and it is something that has come naturally to him. He doesn’t make a luxurious living however, but that’s not his focus. As long as he likes what he’s doing, and can live comfortably on that, he is satisfied.


    Ethan had met Babbu at Eastview University of Virginia (EUV), where they both went to school together. Ethan and Babbu are now close friends and roommates, having shared a two bedroom apartment together for 4 years, and continuing. It’s safe to say, Babbu understands the character of Ethan Turner.


    On the night of the incident, Babbu and Ethan threw a party at their small apartment. The party included karaoke, dancing, and typical party games. Babbu was having a great time at the party. Close to the end of the Party, everyone was sitting down and talking, as the energy in the party started to simmer down. Babbu noticed Ethan was noticeably somber. Chin down and a gloomy impression would certainly indicate his exhaustion. Nonetheless, he began talking to Ethan, with Babbu asking him if he is “ok”, and he knows that he has been losing a lot of sleep recently because of his brother passing from sleeping at the wheel when driving. Ethan told Babbu he would get coffee to energize himself.


    After an unusual amount of hours later from when Ethan left, Babbu received a call from their closest hospital, as Babbu is Ethan’s emergency contact. Upon arrival at the hospital, Babbu was informed of Ethan’s gruesome second-degree burns. Babbu could not ever bear to look at the injuries, due to the severity of the burns. Babbu stayed by Ethan’s side during his stay at the hospital.


    Babbu is a regular customer of MacRonalds, but has never experienced the Robot cashier before. Before the cashier, Babbu has never had one accident occur to the degree of Ethan’s. Babbu loves talking and meeting with the drive-thru workers and misses them.

  • Secret Information:[Don't tell anyone this unless they specifically confront you]

    • On Ethan’s way out of the house to get coffee from MacRonalds, Babbu asked Ethan a question, but Ethan oddly asked Babbu to repeat the question 5 times. Babbu could now clearly see how the sleep deprivation was affecting him negatively, in his regular cognitive functions.

  • Pro-Plaintiff Knowledge:

    • Babbu never experienced accidents at MacRonalds before the Robot, and in-fact misses the drive-thru workers.

    • Babbu saw the severity of Ethan’s injuries, and couldn’t even bear to see them. It shows how badly Ethan was injured.

  • Pro-Defendant Knowledge:

    • Babbu saw Ethan’s unorthodox behavior, from the conversation with Babbu to Ethan asking him to repeat a question 5 times. Babbu knows Ethan was clearly sleep deprived, and this detriment to cognitive function could lead to contributory negligence on Ethan’s part.

    • Babbu knows Ethan's mourns his brother and could have been the catalyst for his lack of sleep in the recent time being. This would lead to contributory negligence.
